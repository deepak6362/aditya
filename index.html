Privacy Policy

Quick Status Saver • Effective Date: 31 January 2026

Quick Status Saver is committed to protecting user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the application handles information and user data.

Information Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data, including but not limited to the following:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • Contacts
  • Messages

All media processing and storage occur locally on the user’s device.

Media Permissions

  • The app accesses device storage only after user consent.
  • Storage access is required solely to detect and save status media.
  • Android Scoped Storage is used to ensure secure and limited access.

Auto Backup & Data Retention

  • Status media viewed by the user may be temporarily stored on the device.
  • All stored content is automatically deleted after 7 days.
  • Users can manually delete saved files at any time.

Advertising Services

  • The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements.
  • Google AdMob may collect anonymous identifiers, such as the Advertising ID, in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Data Security

  • No user data is transmitted to external servers.
  • All app functionality operates entirely on the user’s device.
